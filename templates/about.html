About

DevLists was a small idea that I thought of on a Friday night.

I was frustrated at work when I couldn't quickly find a resource for getting a list of zip codes for a web application I was writing. Then it occurred to me that there wasn't really a website out there that you could utilize to list out information, publicly, for development purposes. While there are wonderful solutions out there like Gist and ix.io, I just didn't feel like these were geared specifically towards my own needs.

If you have any suggestions, don't hesitate to email me: zg@zk.gd.
